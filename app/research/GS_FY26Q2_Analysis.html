Glenn Schorr（Evercore）
Equities 財務槓桿倍增 × 客戶集中度
▼
問題核心
整體市場成交量僅+9%，但Equities毛利率+50%、營收+86%左右，RWA卻反而下降——這組數字背後的wallet share增長從何而來？是否存在客戶集中度風險？
管理層回應
Coleman：equities financing是多年投資成果，去年即決定加碼Asia人才/科技/風控投入，年初監管資本鬆綁後進一步加碼部署；追問客戶結構時，僅泛稱「long-short、量化型等多元客戶」、「大客戶很有影響力但整體高度分散」，未給出具體佔比。Solomon補充：GS在每個地區都具備規模領先優勢，此刻正直接受惠於全球布局的連結能力。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Schorr直接問的是「wallet share增長來源」與「集中度」兩件事，Coleman僅以定性語言（「非常分散」）帶過，全程未提供任何客戶數、單一客戶佔比或前N大客戶營收貢獻的量化數字。GS本身也不在10-Q揭露equities業務的地區/客戶集中度明細（10-Q Note 26的Credit Concentrations僅涵蓋放款，不含交易/融資類客戶曝險），投資人事實上無從獨立驗證「高度分散」這個說法。
逐字稿原文
Glenn Schorr: "I saw overall market volume's up 9%, but margin was up 50%, and your revenue was up like 86% or whatever. Yet somehow RWA is down. Those are all great trends, but maybe you could talk about wallet share gains and what you're seeing in that backdrop."

Denis Coleman: "The performance of our equities business in the last quarter is the result of a number of multi-year investments... on the heels of some of the regulatory capital relief that we received at the turn of the year, identified the people that we were going to deploy more by way of financial resources and try and capture what we viewed as a competitive shortcoming in Asia... We remain very focused around the world for the equities business... trying to identify pockets where we have bigger opportunities to grow our share with clients."

Glenn Schorr (follow-up): "Can you talk a little bit about the client composition? ...if we should be thinking about any concentration risk, because at times there's a handful of big clients that drive that."

Denis Coleman: "The business has different characteristics globally... long-short investors, more quant-oriented investors... We have very big clients in a lot of our businesses, they are very influential, this is a highly diversified suite of clients across the world."
Ebrahim Poonawala（Bank of America）
資本配置優先序 × AI CapEx週期的持久性
▼
問題核心
資本配置在Asia/Europe/US、markets業務 vs AI CapEx融資之間如何取捨，以及本季買回股票的邏輯？延伸追問：Solomon曾公開說市場「貪婪多於恐懼」，如何評估AI CapEx投資泡沫風險與這個週期的持久性？
管理層回應
Solomon：資本配置永遠優先支持客戶業務（如有accretive回報則優先於買回），若無處部署才積極買回——本季屬於「創造更大資本緩衝」的一季，買回與客戶部署同時發生。對於AI CapEx持久性：認為目前仍處建置早期階段，三到五年維度看好，但坦承「不會是直線發展」，未來6-18個月可能出現recalibration，token定價、企業採購方式等仍有高度不確定性。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Solomon自己主動點出「token定價」「企業如何為AI基礎設施定價」等具體風險變數，但完全沒有回頭量化GS自身在這個情境下的曝險——例如若AI CapEx週期在未來6-18個月出現他自己說的「recalibration」，GS的IB backlog（本季創5年新高）、prime/FICC financing餘額會受到多大衝擊，管理層並未提供、分析師也未追問任何情境測試數字。10-Q揭露GS已於2026年4月提交年度CCAR資本計畫，且DFAST壓力測試結果應已於6月發布（法說會前一個月），但管理層在回答這個「壓力情境」問題時完全沒有引用自家壓力測試結果佐證信心。
逐字稿原文
Ebrahim Poonawala: "How you're thinking about capital allocation across your markets business in Asia versus Europe versus the United States, market financing versus all the CapEx AI activity that's going on, and then how you think about all of that relative to the buybacks that we did this quarter."

David Solomon: "It is always our desire to take our capital and allocate it toward supporting our clients... if we could do that and have no excess capital, we would... This was a quarter, given the performance, where we actually created more cushion and more buffer."

Ebrahim Poonawala (follow-up): "Talk to us about the durability of the AI CapEx cycle... you were quoted in the press during the quarter about there's more greed than fear in the market. As investors think about the risk of maybe an investment bubble around AI..."

David Solomon: "We are in the relative early innings of a very, very significant... AI build-out cycle... I'm not smart enough to tell you whether or not there can be recalibrations, in the short term, sometime in the next six months, the next 18 months... There's a lot of talk about token spend and the cost of the technology."
Erika Najarian（UBS）
Asia Hyperscale交易貢獻 × Prime定價權
▼
問題核心
Equities數字爆量，Asia hyperscale交易貢獻多少？近期科技股回檔是否值得擔心？Asia prime capacity據傳偏緊，GS的prime定價是否開始出現議價優勢？
管理層回應
Coleman：equities表現「非常廣泛」，主要驅動是個股相對指數的高離散度（dispersion），在指數上漲同時仍出現高離散度，促使客戶更頻繁尋求GS協助管理部位。Prime方面：確認Asia是刻意加碼的策略選擇，目前已看到部分定價槓桿（pricing leverage）浮現，部分同業也轉趨謹慎，但GS會「保持選擇性」擴張。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Najarian明確問「Asia hyperscale交易貢獻多少」，Coleman完全沒有回答這個具體佔比，改談「dispersion」這個總體市場現象帶過。這是本場法說會第二次（前一題Schorr也問過）分析師試圖量化Asia/單一主題對Equities爆量貢獻度，管理層兩次都選擇不量化——形成一致的迴避模式，而非單一個案。
逐字稿原文
Erika Najarian: "How much is the Asia hyperscale trade driving the equities revenues at Goldman? Does the recent correction, is that any cause for concern?... whether or not in prime you're starting to see a little bit of pricing power, given the global demand for balance sheet."

Denis Coleman: "The activity for our equities business has been very broad-based... we're seeing single name equity dispersion relative to index, and we're seeing that dispersion relative to index while the market's going up... We do see opportunities for pricing leverage. There are some participants in the market that are also, I'll say, being more disciplined."
Christian Bolu（Autonomous Research）
SLR降至同業最低 × 費用效率的可持續性
▼
問題核心
SLR下滑40bp至4.3%，已是同業最低，這對融資業務擴張構成多大限制？願意把SLR壓到多低？另外，H1效率比58.8%已優於60%目標，這是市場強勁的結果、還是結構性降本？
管理層回應
Coleman：公司同時管理CET1、SLR等多重「常常此消彼長」的約束條件，會動態、靈活（nimble）地在客戶機會與資源配置間取捨，但未給出SLR下限數字。效率方面：坦承營收成長40%、薪酬成本僅+30%、非薪酬成本僅+22%，形成明顯營運槓桿，並強調「目前為止沒有結構性的費用基礎改變」——效率提升主要來自營收成長本身，而非成本結構重組。
⚑ 邏輯缺口（原創前瞻風險，見風險矩陣）
Coleman把SLR下滑完全描述為GS主動的資源配置選擇，但依10-Q揭露，GS已於2026/1/1提前採用修訂版eSLR標準，把G-SIB適用的緩衝從固定2%改為「G-SIB附加資本的50%」，使SLR**要求本身**從2025/12的5.0%降至2026/3的3.75%——換言之，SLR下滑有相當比例是監管規則鬆綁帶來的「免費」空間，而非純粹靠GS自己優化資產負債表效率換來的，這個結構性因素在回答中完全沒有被提及。
逐字稿原文
Christian Bolu: "SLR did fall 40 basis points to 4.3%... Your SLR ratio is now the lowest among peers. How much does SLR govern constraints on growing the financing business? Then maybe give us a sense of how low you're willing to run that ratio."

Denis Coleman: "We have a number of different, often oscillating, binding constraints as a firm. We manage to all of them... We will look at the opportunity set that our clients are presenting to us and then make choices about the relative resource allocation."

Christian Bolu (follow-up): "How much of the expense or efficiency gains you've had so far is a function of just a strength in the markets business versus what you've already taken out from a structural cost perspective."

Denis Coleman: "We've been able to grow our revs at about 40%... Comp expense is only growing at 30% and non-comp at 22%... I would not say that there's been any structural change in our expense base at this point."
Mike Mayo（Wells Fargo Securities）
One GS「乘數效應」拒絕量化 × Prime finance供需缺口
▼
問題核心
GS的併購顧問市佔比第二名高出50%，advisory backlog創新高——每賺$1併購顧問費，能連帶帶來多少其他業務營收？是10%、20%還是50%？另外，prime finance需求爆量，GS的產能vs需求現況如何？
管理層回應
Solomon明確表示「不會給你一個具體百分比」，改以敘事框架說明：顧問關係建立信任，信任帶動融資、避險等業務更早、更獨家地找上GS，進而外溢到AWM（更多轉介、更早看到投資機會）與財富管理（客戶財富留在GS內）。Coleman：prime／FICC financing的客戶需求持續「遠超過我們願意承作的量」，這是AI CapEx超級週期下每個地區、每個產業都在找融資的結果。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Solomon是本場法說會中最直接的一次拒絕給數字（"I'm not going to give you a specific percentage, and to be honest, I don't know if I could give you an exact percentage that I could underwrite on a public call"）——但One GS「flywheel／乘數效應」這個敘事在整場電話會議中被管理層主動提及至少5次，作為解釋Equities、AWM、財富管理多項業務同步走強的核心邏輯，卻始終無法被還原成任何可驗證的數字，形成「敘事驅動估值、但敘事本身不可量化驗證」的落差。
逐字稿原文
Mike Mayo: "What is the multiplier effect? For every $1 of merger fee that you generate, how much more do you get in all those other activities... Is it like 10% elsewhere, 20%, 50%?"

David Solomon: "I'm not going to give you a specific percentage, and to be honest, I don't know if I could give you an exact percentage that I could underwrite on a public call... That flywheel is powerful. I can't give you a percentage, but it's a meaningful part of the integrated performance of the firm."

Mike Mayo (follow-up): "Your prime finance is up a gargantuan amount year-over-year. What's your capacity versus the demand?"

Denis Coleman: "There continues to be far more demand across the client segment than we're willing to engage... We're at a moment in time where the demands for the provision of financing are outstripping what we think is the appropriate quantum."
Manan Gosalia（Morgan Stanley）
GBM放款成長來源 × CET1變動拆解
▼
問題核心
GBM放款成長主要來自FICC financing還是併購相關融資？未來balance sheet可以分配多少到這塊？另外，本季VaR上升但RWA卻下降，CET1比率變動的具體driver是什麼？未來一年CET1可望落在什麼區間管理？
管理層回應
Coleman詳細拆解：一部分是併購相關的「deals book」資本部署，一部分是計入FICC financing的其他抵押放款成長；並說明GS現在依商品類型採不同的「取得-結構化-分銷」策略——部分留在自家資產負債表（計入FICC financing）、部分透過Capital Solutions Group的原始發行能力「路由」給AWM客戶（讓AWM客戶取得原本只留給自家資產負債表的曝險）、部分包銷後分銷給機構客戶。CET1方面：市場風險RWA因VaR相關項目下降約20bp，信用風險RWA因特定融資放款活動改善小幅貢獻，其餘變動主要來自獲利留存扣除股利與買回；維持「監管門檻之上50-100bp」的操作模型，目前150bp緩衝高於此區間上緣，多出的部分會優先用於客戶端部署、其餘回饋股東。
⚑ 邏輯缺口（原創前瞻風險，見風險矩陣）
Coleman的回答其實揭露了一個結構性轉變：GS越來越把原本只留在自家資產負債表的信用曝險，透過Capital Solutions Group「路由」給AWM的客戶（同一批高淨值/機構客戶，也是GS財富管理與OCIO業務極力爭取的對象）。這代表AWM客戶正在承接GS自身因CET1／SLR資本限制而無法全部留下的信用風險——管理層將此描述為「不同商品的不同來源、結構、分銷策略」，但沒有任何分析師追問這種「表內資本受限→表外路由給自家財富管理客戶」的安排，是否存在定價公允性或利益衝突的治理機制。
逐字稿原文
Manan Gosalia: "Can you just talk about the strength in loan growth in the Global Banking & Markets business? I guess how much of that is from the FICC financing side... how much of that is from just general deal-related financing?"

Denis Coleman: "We are prioritizing deployment of capital into our deals book... the other collateralized largely relates to what we report as FICC financing... We are using our origination capabilities... and we're routing it to our asset and wealth management business. Because there are clients that we have in asset and wealth management that are very interested in getting exposure and investing in the same kinds of products that we used to invest only for ourselves on our balance sheet."

Manan Gosalia (follow-up): "VaR was up in the quarter... but RWAs were actually down quarter-on-quarter. I guess the question is, what drove that and how should we think about the range in which you can manage your CET1 ratio over the next year or so?"

Denis Coleman: "We remain committed to an operating model that's sort of called plus 50 to 100 basis points. We're obviously in excess of that... You should expect that cushion to be deployed for the client franchise and then some return of capital."
Brennan Hawken（BMO）
人力精簡與AI生產力 × Backlog中的Sponsor回歸訊號
▼
問題核心
營收強勁成長下，人力反而季減2%，是AI／One GS 3.0效率成果、還是刻意的目標？backlog季增年增皆創高，是否看到PE sponsor重新回歸的早期訊號？leveraged finance走強是活動回溫還是主要是再融資？
管理層回應
Coleman：人力數字是「投入與工具運用後的自然結果」，非設定的具體目標，且屬季節性（每年Q3才是大批新人入職季），年增其實是正的、僅季度環比略降。人力科技化提升生產力，讓員工「自然離職後較少感覺需要補人」。Solomon：backlog最大驅動仍是策略性併購，監管環境轉向對「無機式擴大競爭力」更寬容，促使CEO更敢於談大型整併；sponsor對話變多但尚未真正加速，仍是「未來上檔」；leveraged finance部分與AI基建建置有關，也有一部分是sponsor客戶藉由資本結構調整（recap）從被投企業取出資金。
⚑ 邏輯缺口（原創前瞻風險，見風險矩陣）
Solomon把backlog爆量的根本原因歸結於「監管環境變寬鬆，CEO現在敢想大型整併」，這其實是把GS本季的招牌成長故事（「AI驅動的策略性活動」）背後真正的邊際變數，指向了**反壟斷／併購審查政策的寬鬆化**，而非純粹AI基建需求本身——這是一個政策依賴、可能隨政府更迭而反轉的驅動因子，卻被整場法說會的敘事包裝成單純的「AI週期早期階段」，沒有任何分析師追問這個政策寬鬆的持續性。
逐字稿原文
Brennan Hawken: "Despite really robust revenue growth, headcount was down 2% quarter-over-quarter... Is this decline in headcount a function of some of the efficiency efforts you've been focused on..."

Denis Coleman: "I think those numbers are an output of the efforts that we're undertaking, and it's not the result of some specific target... headcount's up year-over-year, but it's down slightly over the course of this calendar year."

Brennan Hawken (follow-up): "Could you speak to maybe some trends in the backlogs, whether you're seeing early signs of sponsor reengaging?"

David Solomon: "We're now also in a regulatory environment where when the question's asked, 'Could I' inorganically enhance my competitive position? The answer is maybe after a period of time where the answer would've been, absolutely no way... While there is more sponsored dialogue, the sponsor stuff still has not accelerated."
Dan Fannon（Jefferies）
另類資產待部署AUM遞延 × H2誘因費用指引
▼
問題核心
非收費型另類資產AUM已超過$1000億，這批資金多久會轉化為管理費收入？另外，管理層預告H2誘因費用「將大幅增加」，能否給出量化區間？這是基於已宣布交易還是估值變動？
管理層回應
Solomon：募資與部署之間存在時間差，且是「階梯式」的連續過程，難以公式化預測部署速度快慢；長期目標是年募資$750-1000億，重點在維持募資機能、讓團隊挑選最佳部署時機。Coleman：確認Q3、Q4誘因費用將「顯著」高於Q2的$112M，且與「已知、有時程表」的特定交易掛鉤，屬於可預期而非隨機事件。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Coleman明確表示這些H2誘因費用「is related to specific transactions that are known and out there」——換言之管理層手上已經有具體交易清單和時程表，卻拒絕具名或給出金額區間，只承諾「materially higher」。這與Mayo提出的「乘數效應」問題屬於同一種模式：管理層掌握可精確量化的資訊，卻選擇僅以定性形容詞回應，讓分析師無法建立可驗證的財測模型。
逐字稿原文
Dan Fannon: "You have fee paying AUM, or I should say non-fee paying AUM, that's well north of $100 billion. I was curious about how to think about the pace of when we should see that kind of flow into management fees."

David Solomon: "There is a lag. You can't formulaically predict it."

Dan Fannon (follow-up): "You mentioned incentive income to increase materially for the remainder of the year. Hoping maybe to get a little bit of a quantification around that."

Denis Coleman: "We do expect in the third and fourth quarter to have materially higher incentive fees. It is related to specific transactions that are known and out there. Based on a schedule and some events, we know that there are incentive fees that we expect to earn as a firm."
Devin Ryan（Citizens Bank）
另類資產募資動能持久性 × Agentic AI客戶端機會被迴避
▼
問題核心
另類資產募資強勁，是長期市佔集中趨勢的延續、還是LP變得更挑剔／更聚焦私募信貸這類特定領域？另外，GS內部AI建置到了什麼階段，agentic AI是否有機會發展成**客戶端**的創新服務、成為GS早期卡位的機會？
管理層回應
Solomon：兩者皆是——長期平台規模優勢持續發威，同時LP確實在特定領域（如私募信貸）更傾向找業績與能力更突出的管理人；募資存在季度間的「lumpiness」但全年方向明確超前。Coleman：agentic AI話題被完全導向**內部**應用——從工程團隊的生產力起步，透過整合大型語言模型供應商關係與外部工程人才招募，逐步把GS內部高度差異化的數據與AI萃取分析能力結合，讓員工提供更好的建議與分析。
⚑ 邏輯缺口
Devin Ryan問題後半段明確聚焦「客戶端（client-facing）」的agentic AI創新機會（"even from a client-facing perspective to be innovating with where you could be early and could be meaningful"），但Coleman整段回答只談內部工程生產力與數據萃取，完全沒有正面回應「客戶端」這個關鍵字所指向的產品化／貨幣化機會，是一次典型的問題錯位式迴避。
逐字稿原文
