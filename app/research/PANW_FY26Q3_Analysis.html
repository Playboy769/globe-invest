$200M AI Frontier Lab 客戶 × 可觀測性/資安整合
▼
問題核心

管理層回應
Lee：兩者仍是各自獨立的專業領域，不能簡單「加一點」就宣稱另一個領域也做得好；未來會透過數據交叉污染（observability數據餵給security、security數據提供context給observability）與agentics逐步整合。

逐字稿原文
Matthew Hedberg: "Great, guys. Very impressed with results, to say the least. Lee Klarich: "Yeah, Matt. I think first of all, it's important to note that these are each specialized environments. Meaning you have to be really, really good at observability, regardless of any potential integration with security, and the same is true with security. I start there because if you look at all of the previous attempts to try to expand from one to the other, what you saw was perhaps a strength in one, but then trying to apply the same logic to the other. In both cases, we have very strong roadmaps of capabilities that we'll continue to add to them. Importantly, vice versa, data collected for the security use case will be valuable to having additional context, broader context for the observability use case. The first part is the data that is collected for each of these independent use cases starts to cross-pollinate to the other. The second part is really related to agentics. What you're seeing across these spaces is a need for AI-driven automated response. Agentics, we believe is that foundation that will be leveraged across all of our platforms, obviously starting with Cortex and extending to Chronosphere. Agentics becomes the other key point where you'll start to see increased integration across the observability and security space from us. Again, in both cases, this is starting from a position of strength independently and the cross-pollination adds to those capabilities."

CyberArk 整合執行力的核心疑問

① CyberArk目前對業績的貢獻不如其他產品（Nikesh原話）

AI 威脅與機會場景

Agentic 攻擊速度：Unit 42模擬前沿模型（如Mythos）可於25分鐘內完成端到端勒索軟體攻擊，遠快於企業平均需數天才能偵測入侵；管理層預期3-6個月內將出現更精密的自動化攻擊者，數年內agentic AI將可全自動掃描環境、產生客製化漏洞利用並執行完整攻擊鏈。

AI資料中心建置：資料中心新建潮推升防火牆硬體與流量檢測需求，帶動NGFW booking創十年最佳單季，同時零組件短缺推升成本與售價（4月已漲價10%）。

Agentic端點生態系：vibe coding工具、MCP servers、hooks/scripts催生「舊端點之上的新端點生態系」，是收購Koi的核心動機。

可觀測性 × 資安融合：AI原生客戶（含前五大前沿實驗室中的兩家）採用Chronosphere，長期將透過agentics與資料交叉污染與XSIAM深度整合，但目前仍是「各自獨立的專業領域」，尚未真正融合。
